Bear Meat Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb bear meat, ground
  • 1 package tortillas
  • 1 head lettuce, chopped or shredded
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions

  1. In a skillet, brown the bear meat over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the taco seasoning and a bit of water. Cook until the meat is well coated.
  4. Serve the meat in warmed tortillas.
  5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, avocado, lime juice, and fresh cilantro.
