Ground Beef and Bean Chili

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the bell pepper, beans, tomatoes, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve hot, optionally with a side of rice or a fresh salad.
