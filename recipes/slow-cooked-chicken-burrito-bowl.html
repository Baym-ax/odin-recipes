Slow-Cooked Chicken Burrito Bowl (For 1)

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cooked rice

Toppings

  • Shredded cheese
  • Avocado
  • Sour Cream
  • Cilantro
  • Lime Wedges, for jucie

Directions

  1. Place chicken breast in a small crockpot. Add the salsa, black beans, corn, chicken broth, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or until chicken is tender and easily shredded.
  3. Shred the chicken with two forks and mix well with the sauce.
  4. Serve over cooked rice and top with shredded cheese, avocado, sour cream, cilantro, and lime juice.
