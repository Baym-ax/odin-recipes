Chicken and Bean Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, grilled or baked and sliced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, black beans, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. Add the sliced chicken.
  3. Drizle with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss to combine and serve chilled.
