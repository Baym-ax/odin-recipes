Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup half-and-half or milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh chives or parsley, for garnish

Directions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into chunks. Place them in a large pot and cover with cold water. Add a pinch of salt.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until they are fork-tender, 15-20 minutes. Drain well.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the butter, half-and-half, and minced garlic over medium heat until the butter is melted and the mixture is hot. Remove from heat.
  4. Using a potato masher or a hand mixer, mash the potatoes until smooth. Gradually add the hot garlic mixture, stirring until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving bowl and garnish with fresh chives or parsley if desired. Serve hot.
