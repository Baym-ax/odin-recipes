Chicken and Rice Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli)
  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and ginger, and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken slices and cook until internal temperature reaches 165 F.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Stir in the soy sauce, cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve over cooked rice.
