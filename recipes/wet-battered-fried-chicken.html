Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

For the Marinade:

  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 Tbsp mustard
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

For the Batter:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 large eggs

Directions

  1. Mix all the marinade ingredients and coat the chicken pieces thoroughly. Let them marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and all the spices. In a seperate bowl, whisk together the water and eggs. Gradually add the wet mixture to the dry, stirring until smooth.
  3. Heat oil to 350 F. in a deep fryer or large pot.
  4. Dip each piece of marinated chicken into the batter, allowing any excess to drip off.
  5. Fry the chicken in batches, making sure not to overcrowd the pot. Fry for about 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and crispy and the internal temperature reaches 365 F.
  6. Remove the chicken from the oil and let it drain on paper towels. Serve, and enjoy!
