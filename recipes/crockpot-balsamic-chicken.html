Crockpot Balsamic Chicken

Recipe Credit: Violet Parker

Crockpot Balsamic Chicken is a flavorful and tender dish made with chicken breasts cooked in a balsamic vinegar and honey sauce. It's a simple and elegant meal that's perfect for all.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Place chicken breasts in the crockpot. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a bowl, mix balsamic vinegar, honey, chicken broth, garlic, basil, and oregano. Pour over the chicken breasts.
  3. Cook on low for 6-7 hours or on high for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  4. Serve garnished with fresh basil.
