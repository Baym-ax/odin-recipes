Shrimp and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deviened
  • 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cups cooked jasmine rice

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, peanut butter, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink, about 2-3 minutes. Remove shrimp from skillet and set aside.
  4. Add bell pepper, broccoli, carrot, and snap peas to the skillet. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return shrimp to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir to coat everything evenly.
  6. Serve stir-fry over cooked jasmine rice.
