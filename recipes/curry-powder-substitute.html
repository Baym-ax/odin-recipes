Curry Powder Substitute

Enjoy the rich, balanced taste of freshly combined spices in this homemade mix, which is perfect for adding a burst of flavor to your favorite dishes.

Ingredients

  • 4 tsp ground cumin
  • 4 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)

Directions

  1. Add all the spices to an airtight container or ziplock bag, and shake well.
  2. Use this mix in place of curry powder in your favorite recipes.
