Copycate Casey's Jalapeno Popper Pizza

Ingredients

  • 8 slices bacon, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 8oz package cream cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1 prepared 16-inch pizza dough pastry
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 jalapeno peppers, sliced into thin rings
  • 1 Tbsp dried parsley flakes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
  2. Place the bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain the bacon and set aside.
  3. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the cream cheese, sour cream, onion powder, oregano, and pepper stirring well to form a smooth mixture.
  4. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly over the pizza dough; top with mozzarella and cheddar cheeses. Arrange the bacon and jalapeno slices over the cheese layer. Sprinkle parsley over the entire pizza.
  5. Bake in preheated oven until crust is firm and crispy and the cheese is browned and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
