Crockpot Sausage Tortellini Soup

Recipe Credit: saltysidedish.com

Try this easy and delicious Crockpot Sausage Tortellini Soup recipe for a simple and tasty slow cooked meal ready when you are.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 2 cups carrots, sliced and quartered
  • 2 cups celery, chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 30 oz. Italian-style diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 16 oz. cheese tortellini, frozen, refrigerated, or pantry work fine
  • 5 oz. frozen spinach
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • Shredded parmesan, optional for topping
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp ground pepper

Directions

  1. Brown sausage according to package directions. Drain grease if desired and place sausage into a 6 quart slow cooker.
  2. Add carrots, celery, onion, spices, canned tomatoes, and chicken broth to the slow cooker and mix well. Cover crockpot and let set on low for 6-8 hours.
  3. Remove the lid and add tortellini, frozen spinach, and heavy whipping cream. Stir well.
  4. Cover and set on high for 20-30 minutes or until the tortellini is cooked.
  5. Serve soup immediately topped with shredded parmesan cheese and a dash of red pepper.
