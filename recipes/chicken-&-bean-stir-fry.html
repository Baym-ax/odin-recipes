Chicken & Bean Stir-Fry

This savory stir-fry combines the convenience of canned chicken and beans with fresh vegetables and flavorful sauces. Quick and easy, this meal is perfect for busy weeknights!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 can of chicken, drained (or 2 cups of cooked chicken)
  • 1 can of beans (any type), rinsed and drained
  • Cooked rice or pasta for serving
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish, optional)

For the Sauce

  • 3 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced (optional)

Directions

  1. In a large pan or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Cook the onions until tender.
  2. Add the minced garlic and curry powder, and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they start to break down.
  4. Stir in the canned chicken and beans, and cook until heated through.
  5. Add the sauce, stirring to combine.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve over cooked rice or noodles and garnish with chopped green onions

For the Sauce

  1. Whisk all the ingredients together in a small bowl until you form a smooth sauce.
