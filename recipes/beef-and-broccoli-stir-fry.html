Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin, sliced thinly
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cups cooked rice

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, peanut butter, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add broccoli and onion to the skillet. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Pour in the sauce mixture and stir to coat everything evenly. Let simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.
