Ground Beef Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded or chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges
  • Shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a medium sized skillet brown the ground beef and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Drain excess fat, then return the meat to the skillet and add 2 Tbsp water and the packet of taco seasoning. Stir until well combined.
  3. Warm the tortillas over a low flame, or in a skillet over medium-low heat.
  4. Assemble the tacos with ground beef, lettuce, tomatoes, onion, shredded cheese, sour cream, and fresh squeezed lime juice.
