Shrimp and Tomato Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onions and cook until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Stir in the cherry tomatoes and basil, and cook until the tomatoes are soft and beginning to break down.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve over a bed of rice or with a side of steamed vegetables.
