Crockpot Apple Cider Roast

Crockpot Apple Cider Roast is a sweet and savory dish made with tender beef slow-cooked in apple cider and spices. Serve with mashed potatoes and green beans for a perfect main course!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 pound beef tritip or shoulder roast
  • 1 cup apple cider
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp paprika
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cloves
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Directions

  1. Mix the seasoning together and rub into the roast.
  2. Place the roast in the crockpot and pour apple cider, apple cider vinegar, and brown sugar over the top.
  3. Cook on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 4-5 hours, until the roast is tender and reaches desired level of doneness.
  4. Remove from crockpot and slice. Serve with desired sides.
