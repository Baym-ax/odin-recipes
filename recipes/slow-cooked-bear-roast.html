Slow-Cooked Bear Roast

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lb bear roast
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Season bear roast with salt and pepper. Place in a slow cooker with garlic, onion, beef broth, and Worcestershire sauce. Cook on low for 8-10 hours.
  2. Serve bear roast with garlic mashed potatoes and green beans.
