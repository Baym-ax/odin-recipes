Bear Meat Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 lb bear meat, cubed
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a large pot, brown bear meat over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion, garlic, celery, and carrots, and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the potatoes, tomatoes, beef broth, thyme, bay leaves, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1-2 hours until the meat is tender.
  5. Remove bay leaves before serving.
