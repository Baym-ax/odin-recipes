Lemon Herb Dressing

This dressing adds a bright, herby flavor that will complement your dishes perfectly

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp honey (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1 Tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a small bowl or jar, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, and honey (if using).
  2. Whisk or shake well until the ingredients are fully combined.
  3. Stir in the parsley, basil, and salt and pepper.
