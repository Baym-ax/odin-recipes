Street Corn Chicken Rice Bowl

Recipe Credit: Erin Jensen

A delicious, flavorful summer rice bowl with marinated chicken and street corn salad!

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 4 small boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tsp worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp lemon zest

Street Corn Salad

  • 2 cups grilled corn
  • 1/3 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 2 Tbsp fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1-2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 cup red onion (diced)
  • 1/4 cup cotija cheese (Crumbled)

Rice Bowl

  • 4 cups rice of your choice (cooked)
  • Black beans
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Jalapeno slices
  • Lime wedges
  • Fresh cilantro leaves
  • Sour Cream
  • 1-2 avocados (sliced)

Directions

Chicken

  1. Combine ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Whisk to combine.
  3. Pour over chicken and let marinade for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Heat grill up to medium heat (approximately 400-450 F)
  5. Remove chicken from marinade and place chicken on direct heat and cook at medium heat for 4-6 minutes per side.
  6. Move chicken away from the direct heat so that it is cooking on indirect heat and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 1605 F.
  7. Cut into slices or cubes.

Street Corn Salad

  1. Heat grill to medium heat. Place clean, shucked ears of sweet corn on a plate or baking sheet. Very lightly drizzle with olive oil. Use your hands to massage oil into each ear of corn. Sprinkly lightly with salt.
  2. Grill on direct heat for a total of 10-12 minutes, turning every 2-3 minutes with grill tongs. Remove when corn is slightly charred. Let cool slightly and then use a sharp knife to cut off the kernels of corn.
  3. Combine corn, mayo, feta, cilantro, lime juice, garlic, chili powder, paprika, salt, pepper, red onions, and cotija cheese in a large bowl. Stir to combine.
  4. Taste and add additional lime juice, chili powder, feta or any other ingredients, as desired.

Rice Bowl

  1. Between 4 bowls divide your rice, chicken, and street corn salad.
  2. Top with additional fresh cilantro, jalapeno slices, and any of the other topping options you want.
  3. Serve, and enjoy!
